Vision Expanded

The Malaysia Diaspora Network (MDN) exists to mobilize the national Church, parachurch agencies, NGOs, academic institutions and practitioners to collaboratively engage in diaspora mission (reverse mission) to enhance effectiveness and impact in light of globalization and transnational migration in the 21st century.
